Mysterio revealed a secret to the world. Now everyone knows that Peter Parker (Tom Holland) is under the Spider-Man suit. Close people support him, MJ (Zendaya) willingly spends time with him and consoles him as best he can. But the hostile world is pressing more and more. Peter decides to seek help from Doctor Strange (Benedict Cumberbatch). Only he can turn back time and make everyone forget who really is under the superhero costume.
